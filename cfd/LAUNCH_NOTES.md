# CFD Launch Notes

This folder is the first public MVP for the project.

## Scope
- CFD prop firms only.
- Company review template.
- Company comparison template.
- Uses existing `src/data/cfd-prop-firms.json`.
- Does not modify SQLite, SQL, or raw CSV source files.

## Deferred
- Futures prop firms.
- Brokers.
- Crypto prop firms.
- Crypto exchanges.

## Pages
- `index.html`: CFD firm directory.
- `company.html`: single company review page.
- `compare.html`: comparison page for up to 4 companies.
